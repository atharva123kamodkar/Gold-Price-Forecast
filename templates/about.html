About Us

Data provided is related to gold prices. The objective is to understand the underlying structure in your dataset and come up with a suitable forecasting model which can effectively forecast gold prices for next 60 days.

This forecast model will be used by gold exporting and gold importing companies to understand the metal price movements and accordingly set their revenue expectations.

In conclusion, a Gold Price Prediction Project utilizing Forecasting Analysis presents a valuable opportunity to gain insights into the dynamic nature of the gold market and provide decision support for stakeholders such as investors, traders, policymakers, and financial institutions. Through a comprehensive analysis of historical gold price data and relevant external factors, forecasting models can be developed to predict future price movements and trends.
